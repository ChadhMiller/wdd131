Brigham Young Univeristy Idaho

Subsequent to my time at Snow College, I made the strategic decision to elevate my academic pursuits by transferring to Brigham Young University Idaho, where I embarked on the pursuit of a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Technology, specializing in the dynamic field of cybersecurity. This transition marked a significant chapter in my educational journey, as I delved into a more advanced and specialized curriculum designed to deepen my understanding of the intricate intersections between computer technology and cybersecurity. During my tenure at Brigham Young University Idaho, the curriculum not only expanded my theoretical knowledge but also fostered a hands-on, experiential learning approach. This immersive educational environment provided me with the opportunity to acquire a diverse set of skills. Specifically, I honed my ability to script using bash and developed proficiency in programming using the versatile Python language. Beyond programming, my educational journey also encompassed an exploration of various operating systems, where I acquired the expertise to navigate, configure, and troubleshoot these systems adeptly. Furthermore, the curriculum exposed me to the intricacies of network setup and design, offering insights into the architecture and protocols that underpin effective network functionality. Classes dedicated to this field equipped me with the practical knowledge required to interact seamlessly with both Windows and Linux terminals. This not only broadened my technical skill set but also empowered me to efficiently accomplish necessary tasks through these terminal mediums, facilitating a comprehensive and hands-on approach to problem-solving in the dynamic realm of computer information technology.
